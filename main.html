MUSHROOM CURRY

List Of Ingradients

  • 3 tsp of oil
  • 1/2 tsp of cumin seeds
  • 1 large onion
  • required water and salt
  • 1 tsp ginger garlic
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 2 tsp chilli powder
  • 1 tomato
  • 250gms of mushroom
  • coriander leaves

Step 1) Heat oil in a pan and add cinnamon stick, cardamom and cumin seeds, after a few seconds
add finely chopped onions, salt and saute until golden.
Step 2) Once the onions are golden add the masalas - Ginger garlic paste, turmeric powder,
Cumin powder, garam masala , coriander powder , red chili powder, green chilies and curry leaves.
Mix all the ingredients well and then add chopped tomato , little bit of water and cook cook until
everything turns soft ( 5 - 6 minutes). You can also add tomato paste to get very smooth gravy.
Step 3) Once oil starts to separate, add the cleaned and sliced mushrooms and mix it well with
the masala mixture and cook for 3 - 4 minutes. Then add required water and cook until the mushrooms
are fully done.
Step 4) Finally garnish with chopped coriander leaves and turn off the heat.
You can add a tsp of ghee on the top to enhance the flavor.
